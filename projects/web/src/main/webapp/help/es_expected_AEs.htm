Enter Study - Expected Adverse Events

  1. Based on the study protocol, determine any Adverse Events (AEs) defined as expected for AE reporting purposes and enter them on this page. This will help determine the appropriate expedited reports required for AEs observed during the study.

    Note:     Make sure to only enter the expected Adverse Event terms that are listed in the study protocol. The study protocol may define expected adverse events for reporting purposes differently than other documentation or clinical knowledge related to the agent or intervention.
  2. Each expected AE term you enter for the study on this page will be saved in the system. If the expected AEs are observed during the study and entered into caAERS, they will automatically be flagged as expected. This information will then be used by the caAERS rules to help determine the reports required for the AE.
