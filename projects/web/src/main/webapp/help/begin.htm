Before You Begin

The administrator must first configure caAERS with data for Organizations, Investigators and Research Staff before information can be entered and saved in the system. Go to the Administration section for more information.

 

Once Organizations, Investigators and Research Staff have been entered into the system by the caAERS Administrator, end users can get started by doing the following:

 

  • Enter a Study

  • Enter a Subject

  • Assign Subjects to a Study

  • Find out if AEs Require reporting
