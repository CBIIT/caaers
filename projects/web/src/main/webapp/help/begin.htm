Before You Begin

The administrator must first configure caAERS with data for Organizations, Investigators and Research Staff before information can be entered and saved in the system. Go to the Administration tab in the main navigation bar to enter information.

 

Once Organizations, Investigators and Research Staff have been entered into the system by the caAERS Administrator, end users can get started by doing the following:

 

General Tasks:

  • Learn About User Roles

  • Enter a Study

  • Enter a Subject

  • Assign Subjects to Studies

  • Enter AEs & Create Expedited Reports

 

Reports & Rules:

  • Enter a Report Definition

  • Import Rules

  • Enter Rules Manually
